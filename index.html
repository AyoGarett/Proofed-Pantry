FAQ

Common questions (we can rewrite these to match your exact approach).

Is Proofed Pantry a medical service?

No. We provide informational signals to help people make decisions. For medical guidance, always consult a qualified professional.

How do you decide the “may contain” risk?

We combine product context, facility/manufacturing signals when available, recall patterns, and other evidence to express a clearer risk estimate.

Do you cover all allergens?

The goal is broad allergen coverage with customizable personal filters. We’ll prioritize the most common allergens first.

Why track seed oils?

Some users want to avoid certain oils for personal preference. We make it easy to identify when they’re present.

Can I advertise on Proofed Pantry?

Yes — we plan to offer ad slots and partner placements. Use the contact form to reach out.
